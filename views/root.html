What

What

busMSN builds on the premise of using open data to simplify public transit for all, regardless of which platform you choose.

Why

Most people use the same handful of stops every day, and know which buses to take, making directions and a map-based interface unnecessary. With busMSN, stops only need to be selected once before the most relevant data is delivered to you in a single click.

The premise is simple: select the stops you use on a regular basis (for example, work and home) and bookmark the resultant page on all of your devices. When you open busMSN, the closest stop to your current location will be displayed with live bus arrival information.
